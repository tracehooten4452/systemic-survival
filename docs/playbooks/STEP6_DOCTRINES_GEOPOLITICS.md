## ATOM COVENANT ☢ — Warden Sievert
Processions: 2/3 irradiated zealots + wardens. Zealots DEATH-BURST vs structures only (≤90px, dmg×2.5).
Dial: turret-dominant casualty memory → the procession walks at your guns instead of the gates.
Counter-play: kill zealots AWAY from your works; bait the burst.

## MERIDIAN GRID ◈ — Director Sol
All laser lances. Timing dial: remembered lockdown habit → audits wait out your shutters (board says so).
A lance dropped BY A TURRET screams its capacitor: nearest gun silenced 2.5s (20s per-gun immunity).
Counter-play: drop lances with troops/hero near your gun lines; vary your lockdown habit.

## THE CHANGED ✷ — The Chorus
Mutant swarm; shepherds stir every wild corpse within 260px — the horde walks with them. Dial: the wave
routes THROUGH the densest wild cluster first ('GATHERING THE HERD FIRST').
Counter-play: thin the wild herds near their approach; noise discipline.

## IRON BULWARK ♜ — Castellan Rook
RETALIATION ONLY: musters solely on war-pact or GRUDGE (demand +3 · assault 3 · encroachment +1;
fades ~5min/point; survives save/load). Shield-heavy columns (60% breakers) using the shared staging/
regroup machinery. Counter-play: give no insult — an ungrieved Bulwark never marches.

## THE DELUGE ≈ — Tidemarshal Okon
The tide: one wave budget split into TWO SWELLS (field pincer + wall swell). Dial: your remembered
shutter speed sets the spacing (fast → 34s to outlast you; slow → 18s one-two). Second swell counted
down on the board. Counter-play: don't spend everything on the first swell.

## RUST KINGS ⚙ — Scrap-King Dozer
Salvage rigs strip your OUTWORKS (barricades, warehouses) and carry the take home — couriers can be
killed before the ledger banks (ash carried-loot rules). Dial: gun-dominant memory → they shop where
your guns don't look (skip targets within 280px of a turret). Counter-play: gun coverage on your
outworks; hunt the couriers.

## GEOPOLITICS (the world without you)
- 28 faction pairs carry a persistent bond (seeded by doctrinal affinity ±35; drifts 2%/6s toward it).
- Bond ≤ −45 = OFF-SCREEN WAR: both bleed pop; escalation outruns drift (wars deepen to a stable
  trough) until one side is ABSORBED (victim pop<4 and under half the victor's): victor takes 60% of
  the pop and +1 str, hard-capped at doctrine+2. Loud milestone; the map consolidates.
- PLAYER LEVERS: a pact with either belligerent steadies the region (drift ×3, escalation halved —
  wars heal in minutes); conquering a belligerent ends its wars outright.
- ALLY POLICY (v0.5.1, locked): an allied faction is NOT immune to absorption — you are WARNED once
  per deep-war episode ('YOUR ALLY IS LOSING ITS WAR') and COMPENSATED on the fall (refugees become
  settlers). Broker or watch it fall.
- COALITIONS require raidEligible(): egress, Meridian lockdown-hold, Bulwark provocation, population,
  and live-wave/pulse gates all apply — a coalition is never announced for a partner that cannot march.
- RIPPLES (all toasted): your alliance warms its friends / bristles its rivals; extortion spreads;
  conquest echoes loudest (friends seethe + grudge, rivals toast you).
- COALITION: a bonded hostile partner whose own wave is nearly due (≤45s) marches with a musterer
  (pull to 8-16s, telegraphed). A distant partner is never pulled — no free difficulty.
